Roadmap for 2024 :


This is a simple cute 16-bit pixel art farming asset pack with animals, farming, and many other tiles in pastel colors.

There are two different packs :

> Basic Pack : 

Includes Grass and water tiles, simple character animations, and many other things. ( gets a few new sprites with each update )

> Premium Pack : 

Includes Everything from the free basic pack plus much more ( Early version available )

Hello, I have a suggestion.

I believe compressing the rear roof into one tile high (maybe into the beam's tile) would make it look more natural.

Assuming the game's camera is at 45° and the roof slope is 30°, then the angle at which the camera "sees" the roof is 15°. Since sin15° ≈ 1/4, a rear roof covering 2 tiles in space should visually appear only 1/2 tile high.
